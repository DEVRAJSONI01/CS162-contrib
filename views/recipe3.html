Whole Wheat Momo Magic

Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 4 (12-15 momos)

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Wheaté Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1/2 cup water (for dough)
  • 1 cup minced chicken or veggies (cabbage, carrots, onions)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Knead Wheaté flour with water into a smooth dough. Rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Mix filling ingredients (chicken/veggies, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, pepper).
  3. Roll dough into small circles, add 1 tbsp filling, and seal into momo shapes.
  4. Steam for 12-15 minutes.
  5. Serve with a spicy dipping sauce.
