Wheaté Noodle Stir-Fry

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 200g Wheaté Whole Wheat Noodles
  • 1 cup mixed stir-fry veggies (broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil Wheaté noodles as per package instructions, then drain.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a pan, add garlic, and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Toss in veggies and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add noodles and soy sauce, mixing well.
  5. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve hot.
