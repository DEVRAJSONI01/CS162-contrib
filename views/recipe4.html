Wheaté Breakfast Flatbread

Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 Wheaté Whole Wheat Flatbread
  • 2 eggs (or mashed avocado for a vegan option)
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp fresh herbs (cilantro or parsley)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Lightly toast the Wheaté flatbread on a pan.
  2. Scramble eggs (or spread avocado) and layer onto the flatbread.
  3. Top with tomatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Fold or slice, and enjoy a hearty start to your day!
